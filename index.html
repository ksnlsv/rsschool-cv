Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Wanted to Ask (and a Bit More)

How do I book a shoot?

+

Super simple – just message me through any convenient platform (Instagram, Telegram, WhatsApp, or the contact form on this site). We'll discuss your preferred date, city, and session type. Once confirmed, I'll reserve the date for you and send over preparation tips.

Can I choose the location myself?

+

Absolutely! You can choose any location you like. I can also suggest beautiful spots based on your preferences and the type of shoot you want.

How long does the shoot take?

+

The duration depends on the package you choose. Mini sessions take about 1 hour, while full Story sessions can take up to 4 hours including breaks.

When will I receive the photos?

+

Delivery time varies by package: Mini - 7 days, Classic - 10 days, Story - 14 days, Wedding - 21 days. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Will you help me pose?

+

Yes! I provide full posing guidance to make you feel comfortable and look natural. I'll suggest poses that work best for you and the location.

What should I wear?

+

Wear something that makes you feel confident and comfortable! I recommend solid colors and avoiding busy patterns. I'll send you a detailed style guide after booking.

Do you retouch the photos?

+

Yes, all photos include professional color correction and basic retouching. Additional retouching services are available if needed.

Can I get the raw files?

+

I don't provide raw files as they are unfinished work. The edited photos represent my artistic vision and quality standards that I stand behind.
